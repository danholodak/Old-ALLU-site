Welcome to the 90 Second Naturalist! Listen and learn something every day about the natural world around you. Thane Maynard is the 90 Second Naturalist and he will introduce you to creatures you’ve never heard of, some you’ve wondered about, and some you thought you already knew. Go to places you’ve never seen, and experience the wonder that is nature, all in 90 seconds!

Over the past 24 years, Thane has written and narrated thousands of 90 Second Naturalist audio segments broadcast on public radio stations across the country. He has been featured on Good Morning America, The Today Show, CBS This Morning, and Late Night with Conan O’Brien.
Thane is the author of thirteen books on wildlife, co-authoring his latest with Jane Goodall; Hope for Animals & Their World. His life mission is to tell the story of biological diversity, natural history, and wildlife conservation to the general public.
As a boy growing up in the days before condos and air conditioning he was exploring the low level swamps of Central Florida. He received a BA in Environmental Studies from Rollins College and an MS Degree from the University of Michigan School of Natural Resources.
Thane is the Executive Director of The Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden. He is also a member of the Board of Trustees for the Margo Marsh Biodiversity Foundation, a private fund headquartered in Washington DC.
